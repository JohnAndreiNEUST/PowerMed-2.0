    Engineering the Future of Hospital Logistics

    A deep dive into how our autonomous fleet integration transformed internal supply chains for a 500-bed facility.

    The Challenge

    Clinical staff were spending 25% of their shift on manual logistics—moving linens, waste, and sterile kits. This led to "nursing fatigue" and delayed bedside response times.

    Our Solution

    We implemented the Sentinel Fleet, a coordinated group of AMRs (Autonomous Mobile Robots) integrated directly into the hospital's elevator and security systems.

    Technical Specifications

    • Core: ARM-based Navigation Suite
    • Network: 5G Private IoT Mesh
    • Safety: LiDAR-based Obstacle Avoidance
    • Uptime: 24/7 Autocharge Cycle
